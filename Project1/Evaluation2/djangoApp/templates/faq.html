Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions we receive about our grocery delivery service.

General Questions

To place an order, simply browse our selection of groceries, add your items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. You can enter your delivery information and payment details before completing the order.

We deliver to most areas within LA. Enter your delivery address during checkout to check if we deliver to your location.

We accept major credit and debit cards, as well as PayPal. All payments are processed securely.

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. Use the tracking number on our website to check the status of your delivery.

Delivery Questions

Your delivery window will be provided during checkout. We offer a range of time slots, and you can choose the most convenient time for you.

If you are not home at the time of delivery, we will leave your groceries in a safe place, or we can arrange a re-delivery based on your availability.

We offer free delivery for orders over a certain amount. Otherwise, a small delivery fee will apply based on your location.
