Privacy Policy

At DeliveryConnect, we are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your data.

Information We Collect

  • Usage data: browser type, device info, pages visited, and time spent.

How We Use Your Information

  • To evaluate your application for delivery positions.

  • To improve our platform and services.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data. We may share your data with background check providers, hiring partners, or legal authorities as required.

Your Choices

You may request access, corrections, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting support@deliveryconnect.com.
